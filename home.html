EAGL LIVELIHOOD FOUNDATION

EAGL, an innovative social enterprise, is dedicated to alleviating poverty and empowering the underprivileged sections of society through the promotion of agriculture-based livelihood initiatives. Founded on the principles of sustainability and community self-reliance, EAGL has embarked on a transformative journey aimed at improving the socio-economic conditions of marginalized communities, especially tribal populations. The organization’s vision is to create a world where poverty is eradicated and every individual has the opportunity to lead a dignified and self-sufficient life. Its mission revolves around promoting inclusive and sustainable development by harnessing the potential of agriculture. By focusing on agricultural practices, EAGL aims to foster entrepreneurship, enhance food security, and generate sustainable income for those at the bottom of the economic pyramid.

Our Aims

Black Gold Rope for Life

Black Gold Hope for Life is a transformative initiative focused on providing goats to rural village families, offering them a sustainable source of income and livelihood. By introducing goat farming, the program aims to alleviate poverty, enhance food security, and empower the most vulnerable communities. Goats, often referred to as "black gold" due to their high economic value, provide multiple benefits including milk, meat, and manure, which can significantly improve the living standards of rural families.

Aman Day Care Centre

Aman Day Care Centre is a pioneering initiative dedicated to providing quality education to children in rural villages. Recognizing the transformative power of education, Aman Day Care Centre aims to bridge the educational gap faced by children in underserved areas, ensuring they have access to the knowledge and skills necessary for a brighter future. Situated in the heart of rural communities, Aman Day Care Centre offers a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive. The centre focuses on a holistic approach to education, incorporating not just academic learning but also essential life skills, health, and nutrition.
